About Us

CodeAbode is a new Chicago-based code academy, designed to turn students into web and mobile app developers. Bootcamp – Full-time weekday and part-time evening options are offered.

The National Museum of Health and Medicine Chicago (NMHM Chicago) and its CodeAbode division uniquely serves - and bridges - physical and virtual communities interested in learning about health, medicine and technology, through museum exhibits, app development, coding courses, and academic and community collaborations.

We are in the midst of an information explosion, where people have access to great amounts of information. There is a great need for CodeAbode, where students can learn a coding curriculum that is critically analyzed to meet the needs of the current, technical job market and for NMHM Chicago, where community members can go for current and research-based health and medical information.

There is a general need for heightened science and digital literacy in a society that is facing substantial technological challenges. In that respect, NMHM Chicago offers a variety of information for the lay audience, and CodeAbode trains people to become information science workers with a focus on health and medicine.

1 ON 1 INSTRUCTION

We make it our priority to provide you with the assistance YOU need while learning. For many of our classes our instructors have a TA that works alongside them, and you, to help you better understand and get the most out of your experience.


AFFORDABILITY

As a 501c3 nonprofit our goal is to make learning accessible to everyone. For this reason we keep our prices as affordable as possible while still providing an excellent curriculum and teaching the most up-to-date skills out there.

Our Courses

HTML & CSS

In our first course, you will learn the foundational tools to create rich and dynamic websites using HTML (HyperText Markup Language) and CSS (Cascading Style Sheets) to create your very own web site.

Intro To Javascript

The most widely used programming language for integrating remote data resources into websites and mobile apps is JavaScript. This course will focus on teaching the you the basics of JavaScript.

Advanced Javascript

Our Advanced JavaScript course builds on the fundamentals of the previous class and introduces concepts used to structure larger programs.

Server-Side

Our Server Side Integration course focuses on creating your own servers allowing you to connect your apps and websites so they can be accessed across the globe.

Java

An introduction to the Java programming language and its object-oriented design approach. Emphasis will be put on aspects of the language most crucial for mobile app programming on the Android platform.

Android

An introduction to programming for the Android mobile platform. Students will learn to program apps using Java and XML, together with the latest integrated visual development tools.

Capstone Course

Our capstone course allows our students to come together and work with instructors on their own individual projects, or in small teams, to develop real world websites and apps that they can include in their portfolios.
