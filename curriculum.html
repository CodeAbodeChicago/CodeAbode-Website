Our Teaching Philosophy

Our curriculum at CodeAbode is built around teaching you how to learn. Programming isn’t about a specific syntax. It’s about a way of thinking and problem solving. All our classes are hands on and designed to guide you to the answering problems yourself.

The Full Stack Curriculum

At CodeAbode, we teach “full stack” web development. We start with the essential ingredients of client-side web development – HTML, CSS & JS. You’ll learn how to plan, design & code an interactive website. From there, we teach server-side programming with Node.js. You’ll learn how to make web apps and come away with a deeper understanding of how the web works. The final course – capstone projects – is about synthesis. You’ll be given the space to design & implement your own web project, with mentorship from CodeAbode instructors.

Full Time Vs Part Time

Our full stack curriculum can be taken as an intensive, full-time bootcamp or as part-time night classes. It doesn’t matter if you take the full curriculum in full-time or part-time form – you’ll still be getting the same hands on instruction for the same cost.

Full-Time Bootcamp

  1. Time commitment: Monday – Thursday, 10 – 4p, 3 weeks per course
  2. Time to complete full curriculum: 15 weeks
  3. Total cost for all five classes: $5000
  4. For the aspiring developer looking to immerse themselves fully and get up to speed as quickly as possible

Part-Time Night Classes

  1. Time commitment: Two nights a week, 6p – 9p, 10 weeks per course
  2. Time to complete full curriculum: about 1 year
  3. Total cost for all five classes: $5000
  4. For the aspiring developer who needs to balance their classes with other responsibilities
