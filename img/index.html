Inventors

DOYLE.VC puts the inventor first by helping them, their idea or their potential startup to move from the current stage to the next stage of development with a focus on the best financial outcome. Our goal is to help good ideas get developed, target a reasonable financial goal in the near-term and reach viability. We help the inventor keep IP and ownership.

Capital

Expertise

DOYLE.VC provides services and infrastructure resources within a small, creative, supportive organization. We provide a nurturing environment for new technology businesses in the Chicago area. DOYLE.VC participants have access to legal advisors, public relations, branding & design, media production, technology advice, financial analysis, pitching, and executive coaching to usher their new business from initial concept through product development, and subsequent funding, with an eye toward a public launch.
