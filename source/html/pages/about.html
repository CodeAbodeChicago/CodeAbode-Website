Non-profit coding school, for everyone.

CodeAbode is the first non-profit code academy designed with the ever evolving health industry in mind. We equip students to become successful web app developers both inside and outside healthcare.

CodeAbode is a division of The National Museum of Health and Medicine Chicago. Together, NMHM Chicago and CodeAbode uniquely bridge physical and virtual communities interested in health, medicine, technology and interdisciplinary education.

We teach “full stack” development – front-end HTML/CSS & JavaScript as well as server-side programming in JavaScript (node.js). Our flexible, hands-on curriculum can be taken in full-time, intensive bootcamp classes or as part-time, evening classes.

Our curriculum doesn’t assume any prior knowledge, so if you’ve been trying to figure out how to get started in web development, apply for one of our upcoming courses!
