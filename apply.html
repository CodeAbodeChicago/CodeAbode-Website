Application process

The process of applying and reserving a seat in a class normally takes 1 - 2 weeks. We want to make learning web development as accessible as possible, so we do not screen applicants. Still, our instructors like to get to know prospective students personally before the classes start, so applying involves a phone conversation with an instructor.

Applications are accepted on a rolling basis, so the earlier you apply the better your chances of getting into a class!
